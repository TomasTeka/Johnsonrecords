Meet the Artists

The Weeknd

Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, known professionally as The Weeknd, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He first gained recognition in 2010 when he anonymously uploaded several songs to YouTube, which quickly garnered a dedicated fan base. His unique sound, blending R&B, pop, and electronic music, has made him one of the most influential artists of the 21st century. His breakthrough came with the release of his mixtapes House of Balloons, Thursday, and Echoes of Silence, which earned critical acclaim and laid the foundation for his rise to stardom. Throughout his career, The Weeknd has continuously pushed boundaries with his genre-defying music, innovative visuals, and dynamic performances..

Over the years, The Weeknd has achieved international success with numerous chart-topping hits, including "Can't Feel My Face," "Blinding Lights," and "Starboy." Known for his distinctive falsetto voice, he has earned several prestigious accolades, including Grammy Awards, American Music Awards, and Billboard Music Awards. His ability to create music that resonates with both mainstream audiences and critics alike has made him an icon in the music industry. Additionally, he has been a vocal advocate for social issues and mental health awareness, using his platform to make a significant impact beyond music.

Burna Boy

Born as Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu on July 2, 1991, in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, Burna Boy's rise to international fame began with his breakthrough single "Like to Party" in 2012. His powerful voice, distinct sound, and the fusion of genres quickly captivated listeners, earning him recognition in both the African and international music scenes.
